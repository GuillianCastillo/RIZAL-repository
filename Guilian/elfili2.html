El Filibusterismo:

El Filibusterismo, José Rizal's sequel to Noli Me Tángere, follows Crisostomo Ibarra's return to the Philippines as the wealthy, vengeful jeweler Simoun, aiming to ignite a violent revolution against Spanish oppression by inciting corruption and exploiting the people's suffering, culminating in a foiled bombing plot and his tragic, confessional death, highlighting the futility of violent revolt and the need for genuine reform.
